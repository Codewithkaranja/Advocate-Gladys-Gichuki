Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions about contacting and working with our firm

How do I schedule a consultation?

You can schedule a consultation by calling our office during business hours or by filling out the contact form on this page. We typically respond to consultation requests within 24 hours during business days.

What should I bring to my first consultation?

Please bring any relevant documents related to your legal matter, including contracts, court papers, identification documents, and any correspondence. If you're not sure what to bring, just bring what you have and we'll guide you.

How much does an initial consultation cost?

We offer a reduced-fee initial consultation where we assess your legal matter and provide preliminary advice. The exact fee depends on the complexity of your case. Please contact us for specific pricing information.

Do you offer legal aid or pro bono services?

Yes, we participate in pro bono programs and consider legal aid cases based on merit and financial need. Please mention your situation when contacting us so we can discuss available options.

How long does it take to get a response after submitting the contact form?

We strive to respond to all contact form submissions within 24 hours on business days. For urgent matters, please call our office directly.

Do you handle cases outside of Machakos and Nairobi?

Yes, we handle cases throughout Kenya. While our physical offices are in Machakos and Nairobi, we regularly represent clients from other counties and can travel as needed for court appearances and client meetings.
