Frequently Asked Questions About Our Team

What legal services does Gladys Gichuki Advocates provide?

Adv. Gladys Gichuki, Managing Partner, specializes in Commercial, Corporate, Civil, and Criminal Law. She provides expert representation, contract negotiation, and alternative dispute resolution for individual and corporate clients.

When was Gladys Gichuki admitted to the bar?

Adv. Gladys Gichuki was admitted to the High Court of Kenya in 2004.

Which legal areas does Advocate Gladys Gichuki cover?

Adv. Gladys Gichuki specializes in Real Estate Law, property transactions, title verification, and conveyancing, ensuring smooth property transfers for clients.

What areas of law does Thiong'o Mboo specialize in?

Adv. Thiong'o Mboo, Legal Consultant at Gladys Gichuki Advocates, specializes in Conveyancing, Real Estate Law, Land Law, and Corporate Law, offering strategic legal solutions for complex client challenges.

How long has Thiong'o Mboo been practicing law?

Adv. Thiong'o Mboo has over a decade of legal practice experience, having been admitted to the bar in 2011.

Who is kavita Ndulu and what is her role?
